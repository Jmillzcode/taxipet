About Us

At TaxiPet, we make pet parenting easier by offering safe, reliable transportation and care services for your furry companions.

Based in Philadelphia, we specialize in pet rides to the vet, groomer, daycare, or wherever they need to go, so you don’t have to rearrange your day!

Founded with busy pet parents in mind, TaxiPet combines convenience and compassion. Whether it's a solo trip or a round-trip vet visit with wait-time included, our services are built around your pet’s comfort and your schedule. We’re more than just a pet taxi—we also offer dog walking and pet sitting, giving you peace of mind while you're away. With clear pricing, door-to-door service, and real animal lovers behind the wheel, your pet is in good hands.
